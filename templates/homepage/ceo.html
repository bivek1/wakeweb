CEO Message

After the foundation of this company, we have come a long way and we have more to go. We started our journey in 2018 and we have spent these past 4 years continuing to satisfy our clients and building our brand; Wake and Tech Nepal has now become one of the top IT companies in Nepal.

What we have done?

In these past 4 years, we have gone through a lot of changes like

  • We have tripled the workforce and have met the demands of the entirety of our clients.
  • We are now a leading IT company in Nepal

I hope this provides you with a few reasons to get to know Wake and tech Nepal better. Whether you are a potential customer, a small business partner, or a future employee, we look forward to finding out how we can work together to bring service to life.
